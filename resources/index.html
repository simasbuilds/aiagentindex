Reusable prompt contract

Give the work structure.

Strong results begin with a clear contract. Copy this framework, replace the brackets, and attach only the context the task actually needs.

GOALHelp me [complete the task] so that [business outcome].
CONTEXTUse [documents/data]. The audience is [audience]. The current situation is [background].
CONSTRAINTSStay within [scope]. Do not [exclusions]. Ask before [approval boundary]. Cite [source requirements].
OUTPUTReturn [format] with [sections]. Flag assumptions, missing information, and anything requiring human review.
QUALITY CHECKBefore finishing, verify accuracy, completeness, traceability, tone, and alignment to the stated goal.
Enterprise guardrails

Move fast.
Keep control.

AI can accelerate preparation and synthesis. Accountability, approval, confidentiality, and business judgment stay with the team.

01

Protect the data

Use approved tools and classifications. Never upload confidential, regulated, personal, or client information without authorization.

02

Verify every claim

Trace important statements to source material. Treat uncited facts, calculations, and recommendations as unverified.

03

Keep approval human

Do not let an agent commit scope, cost, timeline, policy, access, or external communication without the correct owner.

04

Test before scaling

Start with representative test data, define success criteria, inspect failure modes, and preserve a clear rollback path.
